Protect Yourself from COVID-19

What is COVID-19 "new normal"?

As long as the world has not found a cure or a vaccine for Covid-19, we may have to adjust to a “new normal”, meaning a new way of living and going about our lives, work and interactions with other people.

But we have learned to adapt our lifestyles to live with them.

Which brings us to the main question." Are you ready to go back to the cinema?"

If so, below are the Cinema Safety Standard to keep in mind when you visit us. We can't wait to see you at the movies soon!



CINEMA SAFETY STANDARD

CUSTOMER ENTRY

- No entry for guests without a mask on

- No entry for guests above 37.5° Celsius and those with symptoms

- Check-in for tracing purposes via MySejahtera

- Only guests above 12 and below 60 are encouraged to enter

PHYSICAL DISTANCE PRACTICES

- 1-metre physical distance markers at queue areas and alternate paired seating in the theatre

- Alternate concession counters will be open

-Alternate restroom facilities available

- Crowd management procedures

SANITISATION PROCEDURES

- Sanitisation at common touchpoints at least 3 times daily

- Hand sanitisers readily available

- Extended cleaning of auditoriums after each movie

STAFF & FOOD HYGIENE

- F&B are prepared in a hygienic manner

- Staff wear masks at all times

- Staff go through daily health screening & wash their hands frequently
